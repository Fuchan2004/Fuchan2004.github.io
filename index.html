Ocean Biochemistry

Welcome to my personal website! My name is Fadime Renée Stemmer (Pronunciation: /faˈdime ʁeːˈne: ˈʃtɛmɐ/), and I am currently a PhD candidate in the Joint Program in Chemical Oceanography between the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) and Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution (WHOI). I grew up in Southern Germany, obtained my Bachelors degree in Biochemistry from the Technical University of Munich and received a Masters degree in Chemical Oceanography from MIT and WHOI. I am a Biochemist who works on the intersection between structural biology, enzymology, and environmental microbial ecology. Working in the lab of Dr. Mak Saito I work to uncover the proteolytic breakdown of particulate organic matter in the ocean and its contribution to elemental cycling.
